\section{Sequences}

Sum for finite arithmetic series with starting value $a$:

\begin{equation}
    \frac{2a+(n-1)d}{2}\cdot n
\end{equation}

Sum for infinite geometric series:

\begin{equation}
    \frac{a}{1-r}
\end{equation}

Sum for finite geometric series:

\begin{equation}
    \frac{a(1-r^n)}{1-r}
\end{equation}
